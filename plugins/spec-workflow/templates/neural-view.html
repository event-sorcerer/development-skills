Voice settings
Direction
What the visualizer represents: the inbound microphone, outbound speech synthesis, or both at once. Same control as the header toggle.
Microphone
Press to speak: hold Space in the 3D view to keep the mic open. Always on: the mic stays live whenever inbound is shown. Shift+Space toggles between the two. Same control as the red dot in the header.
Echo guard
Mutes the inbound bars while speech output is playing, so the notebook speaker doesn't feed back into the mic (on top of the stream's echo cancellation).
Noise suppression
Mic energy at or below this floor reads as silence — raise it until idle room noise stops moving the bars, without clipping soft speech.
Shimmer
Power of the per-column randomness riding on the bars. 0% = raw spectrum only.
Inbound feedback
How strongly the microphone drives the amber bars.
Outbound feedback
How strongly speech output drives the cyan bars.
Speech test
Speaks the phrase through the active outbound adapter (native system voice) and drives the visualizer's outbound half.
Assistant
On: the picker shows every time this page loads, even if an assistant was chosen before. Off: the last choice loads automatically.
