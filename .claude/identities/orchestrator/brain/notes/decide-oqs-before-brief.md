Resolve every open design question to an explicit recorded pick (or its documented default) on the issue BEFORE briefing the implementer, and fold the decisions into the spec at merge. Up-front resolution costs one comment; mid-build discovery costs a re-brief round. Related: [[frozen-contracts-parallel-prs]].
