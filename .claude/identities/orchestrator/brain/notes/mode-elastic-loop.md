The loop's real strength is mode-elasticity: one session can interleave autonomous batch iterations with human-driven interactive fixes, feature recovery, and live validation — safely, as long as EVERY piece of work still gets its board item, recorded verdict, merge announcement, and retro increment. The board staying truthful is the invariant that makes mode switches free; the moment work bypasses it, the modes stop composing.
