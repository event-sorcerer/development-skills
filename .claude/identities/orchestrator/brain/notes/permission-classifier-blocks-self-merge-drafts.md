The permission system will block converting your own self-authored draft PR
to ready and merging it in the same action when there's no visible human
review step — even under a generic "merge PRs" instruction that didn't
explicitly name or approve bypassing review. This is correct, expected
behavior, not a bug to route around: get explicit, specific merge consent
from the human instead.
